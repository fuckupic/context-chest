## Key Differences from v1 Script

- Opens with multi-tool pain (Claude + Cursor + Windsurf), not just "Claude forgets"
- Shows business context extraction (tech stack + decision), not "remember this" commands
- Emphasizes "I didn't say remember" — the passive extraction
- Shows auto-sort to different colored chests
- Ends with cross-agent promise, not just encryption
- Shorter, punchier

## Tips

- Talk fast-ish but clearly — this is a dev showing a tool, not a tutorial
- Don't over-explain — let the screen do the work
- The money shot is the chests page with colored chests — hold it for 3 seconds
- Face cam in corner is optional but builds trust
- 60 seconds is ideal, 75 max
